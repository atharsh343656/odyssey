About the Fest

Welcome to Odyssey2K26, the grandest inter-college cultural fest.

Introduction

Odyssey2K26 is a premier inter-college cultural fest designed to bring together the brightest talents from across the region to celebrate art, music, dance, and creativity. Our purpose is to provide a vibrant platform for students to showcase their abilities, compete in good spirit, and build lasting connections.

Our Vision

To foster an inclusive environment where innovation meets tradition, and every participant feels empowered to express their unique cultural identities.

Organized By: Department of Cultural Affairs, Vanguard College

Date: 26 March 2026

Venue: Main Campus Auditorium & Open Air Theatre

Why Participate?

  • Win exciting cash prizes and certificates.
  • Showcase your talent to a massive inter-college audience.
  • Network, collaborate, and make unforgettable memories.
